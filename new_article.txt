In its advisory opinion of 8 July 1996, the International Court of Justice found that the influence of the use of nuclear weapons on human civilizations and environment could be totally destructive, while the States are independent and sovereign, which means they do have rights to use nuclear weapons for self-defense in armed conflicts under extreme circumstances. So each case involving the use of nuclear weapons needs to be considered separately depending on its own circumstances.
